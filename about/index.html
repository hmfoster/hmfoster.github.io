About Hailey Balestra-Foster

It's probably fairly obvious this blog has been untouched for years. So. Quick updates: Through Hack Reactor I learned that I love coding, but I couldn't quite get rid of the teaching bug. So now I do both! I've been working as the Campus Lead for Hack Reactor's Remote program for the last several years and can't imagine a job that's a better fit for me, especially now that we're part of Galvanize. What's better than getting to guide people through the same transformation I made, impacting lives in a meriad of ways while problem solving each and every day?

What plans do I have for this blog now? 100% unclear, but I figure I pay for the domain so I probably should find something useful to do with it. Stay tuned for more!
